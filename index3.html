How it Works

HOW TO SELL YOUR CAR IN JUST FOUR EASY STEPS

You can sell your car for more cash in less time by using Cars for Cash. All it takes is four easy steps.

Step 1: Just Contact Us

Car sellers are amazed at how easy it is to sell their car to Cars
for Cash. The first step is to simply give us a phone call at (619)
464-1717 or send us an email that describes the model, year, and
condition of the vehicle. We will buy any car – even the most
battered car won’t faze us!

Step 2: Recieve an Offer

Once you’ve contacted us and told us about your car, we’ll offer
you a fair price for your car. This price is based on a number of specifications,
such as the model, make year, and mechanical condition of the car. If you
approve of this price, then Step 3 will come into play.

Step 3: Schedule an Appointment

We’ll drop by to pick up your car at your convenience. Simply
let us know what time works best for you, and we’ll be sure to
accommodate!

Step 4: Get Paid Cash

You will receive a full cash payment for your car – on the spot. Just have your
title in hand and you’re all set to sell us your car!
